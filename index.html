Scope of Work

We’ll visit your childcare center three times per week, dedicating 6 hours per visit to the following areas and tasks:

  • Children’s Rooms (4 per visit, rotating): With 7 children’s rooms in total, we’ll clean 4 each visit on a rotating basis to ensure all are maintained regularly. Tasks include:
    • Sweeping, vacuuming, and mopping floors
    • Wiping down and sanitizing surfaces (tables, chairs, shelves)
    • Sanitizing toys and play equipment
    • Emptying and sanitizing trash bins
    • Cleaning benches (if present)
    • Cleaning window sills and internal windows
  • Staff Room:
    • Vacuuming floors
    • Dusting surfaces
    • Emptying trash bins
    • Cleaning window sills and internal windows
  • Foyer:
    • Sweeping, vacuuming, and mopping floors
    • Wiping down surfaces
    • Emptying trash bins
    • Cleaning glass doors and internal windows
    • Cleaning window sills
  • Manager’s Office:
    • Vacuuming floors
    • Dusting surfaces
    • Emptying trash bins
    • Cleaning window sills and internal windows
  • Planning Office:
    • Vacuuming floors
    • Dusting surfaces
    • Emptying trash bins
    • Cleaning window sills and internal windows
  • Benches, Window Sills, and Windows: These will be cleaned across all specified areas as applicable, ensuring a polished finish throughout.

Our team is committed to upholding the highest hygiene standards, especially critical in a childcare setting.
